Toys-Hate-Us

A single-player 3D horror game.

Play the Game

Toys-Hate-Us: The Tech Stack

In this project I primarily used the Unity Engine to create the 3D environment in which the game takes place. For the functionality of the game I used C#. Everything from the attack mechanisms to operating the flashlight are controlled by C# scripts. I used BitBucket for source control management and Sourcetree to track branches and avoid merge conflicts with my fellow developers.

Toys-Hate-Us: The Game's Story

The game starts with a series of text messages from our main character's mom. She asks that we look out for our little brother, and to get home before dark. After the intro we are prompted to search the mall for our little brother while little clues guide us along the way. We occasionally run into a pink bear who chases us through the mall and tries to catch us. If you manage to get through the mall alive, you'll find your little brother and win the game.
