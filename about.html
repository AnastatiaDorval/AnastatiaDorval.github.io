My Intro to Computer Science

When I originally started at Knox College, I wanted to be an Electrical Engineer. I really loved my AP Physics class, as well as AP Calculus. Knox's engineering program required a few Computer Science courses, which is what pushed me into CS to being with. After my first CS intro class I just kept signing up because I couldn't get enough of the sort of endless possibilites one had with a bit creativity and some programming knowledge.

My Interests

From a career standpoint I am very interested in working with cutting edge technology such as Ethereum (or other emerging blockchain platforms). In my current role I research and develop smart contracts. On a more personal note, I am really interested in the Metaverse and the infrastructural changes that will need to come in order to deliver real time rendered virtual worlds. This next phase of the internet, Web3 so to speak is reliant on emerging technological solutions and innovations such as blockchain.
