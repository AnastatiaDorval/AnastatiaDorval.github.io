My Intro to Computer Science

When I originally started at Knox College, I wanted to be an Electrical Engineer. I really loved my AP Physics class, as well as AP Calculus. Knox's engineering program required a few Computer Science courses, which is what pushed me into CS to being with. After my first CS intro class I just kept signing up because I couldn't get enough of the sort of endless possibilites one had with a bit creativity and some programming knowledge.

I would like to continue working with and around blockchain technology and reintroduce myself to 3D graphics and the Unity engine.
